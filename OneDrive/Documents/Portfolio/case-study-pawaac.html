PAWAAC: Marketing a Tech Giant.

Redesigned from scratch in Framer, the official PAWAAC product website is architected for premium brand storytelling and high-intent user conversion. Built for a world of autonomous precision.

Role
Product Designer
Duration
6 Weeks
Tools Use
Framer, Figma

01 / The Mission

Telling the Story of Tech.

When I was approached to redesign the PAWAAC website, the challenge was clear: move away from a traditional dashboard-style landing page towards a cinematic, high-end product experience. I focused on architecting a narrative that explains complex drone tech in an intuitive, premium way.

02 / Discovery

Simplifying Complexity.

Before moving to Figma, I conducted stakeholder interviews to map out the "Technical Persona." We discovered that our users aren't just looking for specs—they're looking for operational confidence.

Insight Design Solution
Cognitive Overload Minimalist data visualization and layered disclosure.
Trust in Precision Micro-animations signifying data processing speed.
Brand Authority High-contrast, brutalist-industrial typography.
03 / Design Logic

Architecture of Trust.

Defense tech often feels cold and intimidating. I chose to blend industrial aesthetics with "human-first" interaction models. If an operator trusts the interface, they make fewer tactical errors.

  • Blueprint Grid: Established a strict mathematical layout that feels engineered and reliable.
  • High-Contrast HUD: Optimized for visibility in low-light environments (night surveillance).
  • Fluid Interactions: Using Framer’s motion engine to signify data flow transitions.
04 / Product Scalability

A Modular Component System.

Because PAWAAC is an evolving tech family, the website needed to scale. I developed a design system of 40+ unique components in Framer, allowing the marketing team to launch new landing pages for various drone models in hours rather than weeks.

05 / The Technical Hurdle

Performance at Scale.

The primary challenge was balancing high-fidelity video background assets with web performance. I implemented a custom lazy-loading strategy and video optimization pipeline that reduced page load times by 40% without sacrificing visual crispness.

06 / Outcome

Mission Successful.

The redesign transformed a complex dashboard into a streamlined command center. The result is a platform that isn't just "functional"—it's a premium experience that respects the user’s cognitive load.
